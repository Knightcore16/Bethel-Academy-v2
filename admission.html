Admission

Welcome to Bethel Methodist Academy!

Thank you for your interest in Bethel Methodist Academy. We are excited to invite you to be a part of our vibrant and inclusive educational community. Our admissions process is designed to ensure a fair and transparent selection of students who share our commitment to academic excellence and personal growth.

Why Choose Bethel Methodist Academy?

At Bethel Methodist Academy, we strive to provide a holistic education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, creativity, and character development. Here are some of the reasons why families choose us:

  • Experienced and dedicated faculty
  • Rigorous academic programs
  • State-of-the-art facilities and resources
  • Wide range of extracurricular activities
  • Supportive and inclusive learning environment

Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to assess each applicant's potential and suitability for Bethel Methodist Academy. Here's an overview of the steps involved:

  1. Step 1: Submit an Application
    Complete our online application form, providing accurate and up-to-date information about the student's academic background, extracurricular involvement, and personal achievements.
  2. Step 2: Entrance Assessment
    Eligible applicants will be invited to participate in an entrance assessment, which may include written tests, interviews, or other evaluation methods to gauge the student's aptitude and potential.
  3. Step 3: Interview
    Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a personal interview with members of our admissions team. This allows us to learn more about the student, their interests, goals, and how they align with the values of our school.
  4. Step 4: Notification of Decision
    Once the evaluation process is complete, applicants and their families will be notified of the admission decision within a reasonable timeframe. Accepted students will receive further instructions for enrollment.
